STACK

Next to Sint Pietersplein, the land with an elevation difference of 4 meters at 120 meters is at the end of Overpoort street. The idea presented about settling next to an urban void is concerned with being an addition, a pocket to this void, and with a park on the street side.
Site: Ghent, BE

Stack can be increased, decreased, removed or open to different scenarios. Although the main material is timber, polycarbonate is considered for indoor installation for office, library and similar uses. Considering the main materials of the facades, stored products, and vertical gardens, these additions are far from examples, they are design decisions and are from today. The rhythm in the example of grindbakken was used in the planning of the site, and with a similar logic, meeting points, and courtyards were created at the corners.
